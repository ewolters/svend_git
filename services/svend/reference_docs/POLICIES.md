## 1. Acceptable Use Policy

### 1.1 Permitted Uses
Svend is designed for:
- Educational problem-solving (math, physics, chemistry, logic)
- Learning assistance and tutoring
- Code debugging and explanation
- General reasoning and analysis tasks

### 1.2 Prohibited Uses
Users may NOT use Svend for:

**Harmful Content**
- Generating malware, exploits, or attack code
- Creating content that promotes violence or harm
- Producing illegal content of any kind
- Harassment or abuse of individuals

**Academic Dishonesty**
- Submitting Svend outputs as your own work without disclosure
- Circumventing academic integrity policies
- Taking exams or assessments on behalf of others

**Deception**
- Impersonating humans or other AI systems
- Generating misinformation or disinformation
- Creating fake reviews, testimonials, or endorsements

**System Abuse**
- Attempting to extract model weights or training data
- Automated scraping or excessive API calls
- Circumventing rate limits or authentication
- Reverse engineering the service

### 1.3 Enforcement
Violations may result in:
1. Warning (first offense, minor violations)
2. Temporary suspension (repeated or moderate violations)
3. Permanent ban (severe violations)
4. Legal action (illegal activity)

## 2. Privacy Policy

### 2.1 What We Collect

**Data We DO Collect**:
| Data Type | Purpose | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Email address | Account identification | Until account deletion |
| API key (hashed) | Authentication | Until revoked |
| Request timestamps | Rate limiting, debugging | 90 days |
| Token counts | Usage tracking, billing | Indefinite |
| Error logs | Debugging | 30 days |
| IP addresses | Abuse prevention | 30 days |

**Data We DO NOT Collect**:
- Your prompts/questions
- Model responses
- Content of your conversations
- Personal information beyond email

### 2.2 How We Use Data
- Authenticate your requests
- Track usage for billing
- Prevent abuse and ensure fair access
- Debug errors and improve service
- Comply with legal requirements

### 2.3 Data Sharing
We do NOT:
- Sell your data
- Share data with advertisers
- Use your data to train models
- Share data except when legally required

### 2.4 Your Rights
You may:
- Request a copy of your data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Export your usage history
- Opt out of non-essential data collection

Contact: privacy@svend.ai

### 2.5 Data Security
- All data encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- All transmissions encrypted (TLS 1.3)
- API keys stored as salted hashes
- Regular security audits
