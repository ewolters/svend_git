What ISO 9001 clauses does Svend cover?
All 32 clauses from 4.1 (Context of the organization) through 10.3 (Continual improvement). Every clause is fully active with CRUD, filtering, and analytics. See the full table above.
Do I need separate QMS software if I use Svend?
No. Svend's QMS module is built into the same platform you use for SPC, DOE, capability analysis, and root cause analysis. NCR tracking, internal audits, training records, management review, and document control are all native features on Team and Enterprise plans. No duplicate data entry or third-party integrations required.
How does Svend handle nonconformance tracking for ISO 9001?
Full CAPA workflow per clause 10.2. Log NCRs with severity classification (minor, major, critical) and ISO clause reference. Record containment actions, conduct root cause analysis using 5 Whys or fishbone diagrams, define corrective actions with owners and due dates, verify effectiveness, and close. Every NCR traces to its root cause analysis and any linked FMEA failure mode.
Can Svend schedule and track ISO 9001 internal audits?
Yes, per clause 9.2. Create audit programs with lead auditors, department scoping, and ISO clause mapping. Record findings as observations, minor nonconformities, major nonconformities, or opportunities for improvement. Each finding generates corrective actions tracked to closure with verification.
Does Svend include a training matrix for ISO 9001 competence requirements?
Yes, per clause 7.2. Define competence requirements by role, track employee training records with completion dates and expiry dates, monitor compliance rates per ISO clause, and receive alerts before certifications expire. The training matrix integrates with the learning module for course delivery and assessment.
How does Svend handle document control for ISO 9001?
Per clause 7.5. Controlled document register with document numbers, categories, version tracking, and approval workflows. Schedule reviews, track revisions with change summaries, and maintain a full audit trail. Obsolete versions are automatically archived. Supports quality manuals, SOPs, work instructions, and forms.
Can Svend manage supplier quality for ISO 9001 clause 8.4?
Yes. Supplier register with approval status, quality ratings, evaluation scheduling, and product/service records. Track approved supplier lists, schedule periodic re-evaluations, and link supplier performance data to incoming inspection NCRs. Integrates with the NCR tracker for externally provided product nonconformances.
