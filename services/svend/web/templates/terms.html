Section 5

Data Security & Compliance

We take data security seriously. SVEND is built to meet the expectations of organizations operating under ISO 9001:2015 and similar quality management standards.

  • Encryption at rest — sensitive data fields are encrypted with AES-128 (Fernet) before database storage, including conversations, analyses, uploaded files, and billing identifiers
  • Encryption in transit — all connections use HTTPS with HSTS (2-year max-age, preload)
  • Private infrastructure — SVEND runs on dedicated, self-hosted servers, not shared cloud instances
  • Database isolation — PostgreSQL on localhost with no external database access
  • Audit trail — all analyses, edits, and decisions are timestamped and attributable for ISO 9001 traceability requirements
  • Password hashing — PBKDF2-SHA256 with 720,000 iterations
  • Daily encrypted backups — AES-256 encrypted backups retained for 30 days
  • No third-party analytics or tracking — we do not use advertising networks, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics services

For complete technical details, see our Privacy Policy.
