Questions

How does this compare to Minitab?
Same statistical rigor: ANOVA, DOE, SPC, capability studies, hypothesis tests. But Svend adds AI that helps you think - it detects assumptions, challenges reasoning, and drafts conclusions. And it's 96% cheaper ($228/year vs $1,500+/year).
What does the AI actually do?
The AI observes your workspace, detects patterns in your data, infers what you're assuming, and challenges your reasoning. It doesn't give you answers - it helps you arrive at structured responses by asking "have you considered..." and "your evidence shows X but you haven't tested Y."
Is my data private?
Yes. Your data stays on our servers (encrypted), never shared, never used for training. Export or delete anytime. Enterprise customers can run on-premises.
