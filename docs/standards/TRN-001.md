## **1. SCOPE AND PURPOSE**

### **1.1 Purpose**

TRN-001 defines the training competency management framework for Svend. It encodes the **TWI (Training Within Industry)** 4-level competency model — rooted in the CCS 1948 → Protzman lineage — into a two-dimensional training matrix that displays **competency level** (Harvey balls) and **certification status** (colored backgrounds) simultaneously.

**Core Principle:**

> A training matrix that only shows "done" or "not done" tells you nothing about capability.
> The TWI model distinguishes between awareness, supervised practice, independent competence,
> and the ability to teach. Harvey balls encode this progression at a glance.
> Certification status (current/expiring/expired) is the second dimension — overlaid as background color.

### **1.2 Scope**

**Applies to:**
- TrainingRequirement — training requirements with ISO clause references and recertification frequency
- TrainingRecord — individual employee training records with competency level tracking
- TrainingRecordChange — field-level audit trail for all competency and status changes
- Training matrix UI — grid and list views displaying Harvey ball competency + certification backgrounds

### **1.3 Lineage**

The competency model traces directly from TWI (Training Within Industry), developed by the Council for Civilian Services (CCS) in 1948, refined through the Protzman integrated framework (TIPS/WFA/SMED), and adapted into the "See One, Do One, Teach One" progression used in surgical training, skilled trades, and continuous improvement:

```
CCS 1948 → TWI Job Instruction → Protzman Sr. → Shingo/Ohno → Charlie Protzman → Eric → SVEND
```

## **3. TERMINOLOGY**

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Competency** | Demonstrated ability to perform a task at a defined level (0–4) per the TWI progression model. |
| **Certification Status** | Computed state (current/expiring/expired/incomplete/not_required) derived from training record status and expiry date. |
| **Training Matrix** | Two-dimensional grid (employee x requirement) displaying competency level (Harvey balls) and certification status (background color) simultaneously. |
| **Harvey Ball** | Unicode glyph (○ ◔ ◑ ◕ ●) representing competency level 0–4 as a filled-circle progression. |
| **TWI** | Training Within Industry — the CCS 1948 methodology encoding a 4-level "See One, Do One, Teach One" competency progression. |
| **Recertification** | Process of renewing an expired or expiring certification through reassessment or retraining. |

## **4. COMPETENCY MODEL**

### **4.1 TWI 4-Level Competency Scale**

Every `TrainingRecord` carries a `competency_level` integer (0–4) representing progression through the TWI framework:

| Level | Harvey Ball | Label | TWI Phase | Description |
|-------|-----------|-------|-----------|-------------|
| 0 | ○ | No Exposure | — | Not yet introduced to the skill or process |
| 1 | ◔ | Awareness | See One | Has observed the process; understands purpose and basic steps |
| 2 | ◑ | Supervised | Do One (supervised) | Can perform the task under direct supervision |
| 3 | ◕ | Competent | Do One (independent) | Performs independently to quality standard |
| 4 | ● | Trainer | Teach One | Can instruct others and validate their competency |

## **5. CERTIFICATION STATUS**

### **5.1 Computed Status**

Certification status is **derived** (not stored) from `TrainingRecord.status` and `TrainingRecord.expires_at`:

| Status | Rule | Background Color |
|--------|------|-----------------|
| `current` | `status == "complete"` AND (`expires_at` is null OR `expires_at > now + 30 days`) | Green (#4ade8033) |
| `expiring` | `status == "complete"` AND `expires_at ≤ now + 30 days` AND `expires_at > now` | Yellow (#facc1533) |
| `expired` | `status == "expired"` OR (`status == "complete"` AND `expires_at ≤ now`) | Red (#f8717133) |
| `incomplete` | `status ∈ {"not_started", "in_progress"}` | Gray (#6b728033) |
| `not_required` | No record exists for this employee × requirement pair | Transparent |

### **6.2 Harvey Ball Glyphs**

Unicode characters used for Harvey ball rendering:

```
○  U+25CB  WHITE CIRCLE         — Level 0 (No Exposure)
◔  U+25D4  CIRCLE WITH UPPER RIGHT QUADRANT BLACK  — Level 1 (Awareness)
◑  U+25D1  CIRCLE WITH RIGHT HALF BLACK  — Level 2 (Supervised)
◕  U+25D5  CIRCLE WITH ALL BUT UPPER LEFT QUADRANT BLACK  — Level 3 (Competent)
●  U+25CF  BLACK CIRCLE         — Level 4 (Trainer)
```

### **6.3 Legend**

The matrix **MUST** display a two-row legend:
- **Row 1:** Harvey ball glyphs with competency level labels
- **Row 2:** Colored squares with certification status labels

## **10. ANTI-PATTERNS**

### **10.1 Certifications Without Expiry Tracking**

**PROHIBITED:** Recording training completions without an `expires_at` date for certifications that require periodic renewal.

Every certification with a regulatory or organizational recertification requirement **MUST** have `expires_at` set on the `TrainingRecord`. Relying on manual memory for renewal scheduling defeats the purpose of the training matrix.

### **10.2 Training Records Without Evidence**

**PROHIBITED:** Marking competency level 3 (Competent) or 4 (Trainer) without attaching artifact evidence.

High competency levels represent independent capability and teaching authority. These claims **SHOULD** be backed by artifact uploads (signed competency sheets, assessment results, certificates) via the M2M `UserFile` relationship.

### **10.3 Bypassing Audit Trail for Competency Changes**

**PROHIBITED:** Directly updating `competency_level` on a `TrainingRecord` without creating a corresponding `TrainingRecordChange` entry.

All competency level transitions — including corrections and administrative overrides — **MUST** flow through the tracked update path that generates audit entries. Direct ORM writes that skip the change tracking mechanism violate AUD-001.
